About Resume Maker

Markdown resume builder with live preview and PDF export

Resume Maker is a free browser-based resume builder for people who prefer writing in Markdown and exporting polished resumes without signing up. You can draft content fast, preview multiple templates, adjust styles visually, and export PDF or HTML directly from the browser.

Write in Markdown

Use a plain text workflow for experience, education, and projects instead of fighting form fields.

Preview templates instantly

Switch templates and styles while keeping the same content, so you can compare different resume looks quickly.

Keep data local-first

Your draft stays in the browser by default, which is useful when you want private editing without an account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to use Resume Maker?

No. Resume Maker works directly in the browser and does not require sign-up before editing.

Can I export my resume as PDF?

Yes. You can export PDF or HTML from the browser after reviewing the live preview.

Can I use Markdown with Resume Maker?

Yes. The editor is designed around Markdown, so you can keep your resume in a portable text format.
